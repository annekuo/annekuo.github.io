Usability suggestions for Delta: a retro game emulator

Delta is a free iOS emulator for retro video games. It runs games from older game consoles like Game Boy Advance, Nintendo DS, and more. Users can enjoy their favorite childhood games without jailbreaking. It also allows users to save game progresses in multiple files and resume playing one of them of their choice. This article will critique some of Delta’s main features based on Don Norman’s Fundamental Principles of Interaction.
