Project Summary

This project was a submission to the 2023 Product Design Student Challenge hosted by Amazon Music and Pratt iSchool. In our submission, we explored possible features in the future for a music streaming app catered towards Gen-Z listeners.

Drawing from our survey (57 respondants) and interviews (8 interviewees), we synthesized three insights of our users' needs:

  • Emotional & Environmental Detection
  • Personalization
  • Social Interactions

Combining AI and the collection user data, We were able to develop 4 features that provides personalized music streaming experience that values the emotions of our users. These features include:

  1. Emotion detection connected with AI generated playlist
  2. Now-playing background that reflects user emotion
  3. Personal and World Emotion Trend
  4. Built-in social tab

Looking back onto this project, we were trying to fulfill too many different needs and did not dive deeper into one idea. If I could do this again, I would focus more on 1-2 insights, develop prototype and conduct a usability test to refine the features.

Research

Survey Results

User Persona

Based on our survey and 8 interviews, we were able to illustrate our target users in this persona:

Pain Points

Hard to recall what music she played

“I remember I was sad and I listen a lovely lullaby... but when was it?”

Recommended songs does not align with her taste

“I mean, this is a cheerful playlist. But I wish there were more instrumental songs in it.”

Switching between multiple apps just to share songs

“I’m just switching multiple times whenever I want to share a song. It gets pretty annoying.”

Ideation

With our users and their pain points in mind, we brainstormed based on these HMW statements:

01

How might we detect users’ emotions and environment so they can receive songs that match their current situations?

02

How might we link users’ personal emotional journeys with their music library?

03

How might we make sharing songs with friends intuitive and convenient?

Prototype

01

Emotion detection on launch

Combining facial recognition and heat rate measurement, the algorithm will detect users' emotions.
If the detected mood doesn't match with their feelings, users can adjust it in the homepage for better song recommendations.

02

Reflect how well the song matches

user can drag the small circle on the ring surrounding the album image to indicate whether the song matches their mood.

03

AI-generated playlist based on mood and environment

Personalized playlists are generated from the data available, including user emotion, favorite genres, and current weather, temperature, and time for an enjoyable listening experience. Users can change the environment or the emotion to generate a new list.

04

Emotional listening history

Users are able to see their emotional changes in the listening history. They can easily find songs they enjoyed in thier ups and downs, and would have less problem recalling the date and time in order to find those songs again.

05

World emotional trend

Users can explore the emotional trends of different countries, and the collective emotion of each county is represented with a color. This emotional reflection can be an indication of big news happening in the country.

06

Friend activity and chatrooms

Users can easily see what their friends are listening to, and instantly share songs they like with each other without copy and pasting it to a third-party application.
