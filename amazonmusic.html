Project Summary

This project was a submission to the 2023 Product Design Student Challenge hosted by Amazon Music and Pratt iSchool. In our submission, we explored possible features in the future for a music streaming app catered towards Gen-Z listeners.

Drawing from our survey (57 respondants) and interviews (8 interviewees), we synthesized three insights of our users' needs:

  • Emotional & Environmental Detection
  • Personalization
  • Social Interactions

Combining AI and the collection user data, We were able to develop 4 features that provides personalized music streaming experience that values the emotions of our users. These features include:

  1. Emotion detection connected with AI generated playlist
  2. Now-playing background that reflects user emotion
  3. Personal and World Emotion Trend
  4. Built-in social tab

Looking back onto this project, we were trying to fulfill too many different needs and did not dive deeper into one idea. If I could do this again, I would focus more on 1-2 insights, develop prototype and conduct a usability test to refine the features.
